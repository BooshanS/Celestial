The Know and The How

Celestial Bohemia is a combination of art, story and world that will be powered by the technology of blockchain and cryptocurrency
Celestial Bohemia is on track to create following products: NFTs, Comics, Merchandises and Metaverse as part its roadmap
The first product from Celestial Bohemia will be the first of its NFT collections – “The Light and The Vanta”. This is planned to be launched in June 2022.
You can follow the storyline on Twitter @Celest_Boh. The owners of the NFT will have free access to the full story line as they are released.
While Celestial Bohemia aims be swift to adapt to the ever changing technological feats in the blockchain and cryptocurrency space, it aspires to build a platform for its own use and scale it by onboarding the peers in the industry.
Celestial Bohemia wishes to feed the imagination of the next generation of comic enthusiasts. Enable the entire industry by building an in-house platform for creators, artists and new thinkers bring their creations to life in the world we aspire to build.
